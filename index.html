Short Bio

​I am currently a first year Ph.D. student in the Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Prof. John W.Z. SHI. Previously, I received the M. Eng. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen in 2022, under the supervison from Prof. Max Q.-H. Meng. I graduated from Nanjing Tech University as a Bachelor in 2020, supervised by Prof. Lihang Feng.

Research Interests
  • LiDAR-based SLAM, Point Cloud Registration, Scene Understanding, Mobile Mapping System
